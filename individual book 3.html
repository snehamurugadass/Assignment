YOU ARE UNIQUE

Author:A P J ABDUL KALAM Author Name 3

Description:‘Strive to be unique,' Abdul Kalam writes in the book You Are Unique, that the world is transformed by those who 'walked into the unexplored path and strived to be unique. The human challenge is to fight the hardest battle of life till one arrives at one’s Unique self because 'the world around you is trying its best to make you just everyone else.'
